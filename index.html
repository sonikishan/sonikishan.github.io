I went to her home with family. As Mom had gone to USA, Mama & Mami and Dad were there at home. It was really good day for me seeing her after many days. She was feeling bit of shy. We all talked for hours and enjoyed

10 July: Almost after half year we meet again !!

Almost after half year we meet again !! She came to my home with family. We went near garden to sit and talk, it was really good feeling talking to her after such a long time.

This was the day i got to know she knows one of my best friend (Hemant), Infact they studied in same school during childhood.

14 July: First meet alone @Anand !!

Our first meet alone at Anand sardar patel garden. We were little nervous on this meet. During this time i was working in Baroda so i had gone to Anand via bus, she had come to pick me.

At garden we had quality time and discussed so many things. Later at night we went for dinner at Anand hotel near high way. I went back to Baroda via train !!

Very next day we told yes to our parents.
